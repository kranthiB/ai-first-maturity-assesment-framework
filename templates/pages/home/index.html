AFS Maturity Levels

Understand your organization's current position and roadmap for AI-first excellence.

Level 1
Traditional Development

AFS Score: 1.0-1.7

Minimal or no AI integration in development processes. Teams rely primarily on traditional manual methods with basic development tools and practices.

Level 2
AI-Assisted Development

AFS Score: 1.8-2.4

Basic AI tool adoption with individual usage patterns. Teams begin incorporating AI assistance for specific tasks like code completion and basic documentation.

Level 3
AI-Augmented Development

AFS Score: 2.5-3.2

Systematic AI integration across development workflows. Teams have standardized AI practices with coordinated usage and measurable productivity improvements.

Level 4
AI-First Development

AFS Score: 3.3-4.0

Advanced AI-native development with autonomous capabilities. Teams leverage intelligent systems for most development activities with predictive and self-improving processes.
